Code of Conduct

Our Pledge

We are committed to creating a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone who participates in this project.

Our Standards

Expected Behavior
	•	Be respectful and kind to others
	•	Respect different opinions and experiences
	•	Give constructive feedback
	•	Take responsibility for mistakes
	•	Act professionally in all interactions

Unacceptable Behavior
	•	Harassment, discrimination, or hateful behavior
	•	Offensive, insulting, or threatening comments
	•	Sharing private information without permission
	•	Any behavior that is inappropriate in a professional setting

Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct and may take action against inappropriate behavior.

Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including online discussions, code contributions, and official community events.

Enforcement

Violations may be reported to the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed fairly and confidentially.

Possible actions include:
	1.	Warning
	2.	Temporary restriction
	3.	Temporary ban
	4.	Permanent ban

Disclaimer

Maintainers will act in good faith to protect the community and ensure a respectful environment.

Attribution
